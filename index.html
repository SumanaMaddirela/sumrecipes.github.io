Recipe #1 Paneer Butter Masala

  • Time:35 minutes
  • Serves:4

For Onion Tomato Paste

Ingredients Quantity
Butter 1 tsp
Oil 1 tsp
Ginger 1 inch
Garlic 3 Cloves
Onion 1(large)
Tomato 2(large)
Cashew/Kaju 10

Other Ingredients

Ingredients Quantity
Butter 2 tbsp
Cardamom 2 pods
Bay leaf 1
Turmeric 1/4 tsp
Kashmiri Red chilli powder 1 tsp
Garam masala 1/4(first)+1/4(later) tsp
Cumin Powder 1/4 tsp
Water 1 cup
Sugar 1 tsp
Salt 1 tsp
Cream 2 tbsp
Paneer 20 cubes
Coriander 2 tbsp(chopped)
Kasuri methi 1/2 tsp(crushed)

Steps for making:-

Step 1: firstly, in a large kadai heat 1 tsp butter and 1 tsp oil.
Step 2:saute 1 onion, 1 inch ginger and 3 clove garlic until it shrinks slightly.
Step 3:now add 2 tomato and 10 cashew for a minute.
Step 4:cover and cook for 10 minutes or until tomatoes turn soft and mushy. Step 5:cool completely and transfer to the blender. Step 6:blend to smooth paste adding water if required. Step 7:in a kadai heat 2 tbsp butter and saute 2 pods cardamom and 1 bay leaf. Step 8:keeping the flame on low add ¼ tsp turmeric, 1 tsp chilli powder, ¼ tsp garam masala and ¼ tsp cumin powder. Step 9:saute until the spices turn aromatic without burning. Step 10:add in prepared onion tomato paste and saute well. Step 11:saute until oil separates from sides. Step 12:further add 1 cup water, ½ tsp sugar and 1 tsp salt. Step 13:mix well adjusting consistency as required. Step 14:also, add 2 tbsp cream and mix well. Step 15:now add 20 cubes of paneer and mix gently. Step 16:cover and simmer for 10 minutes or until flavours are absorbed. Step 17:further add 2 tbsp coriander, ½ tsp kasuri methi and ¼ tsp garam masala. mix well. Step 18:finally, enjoy paneer butter masala with roti or naan.

Source: Paneer butter masala recipe website

Recipe #2 Maggie

  • Time:5 minutes
  • Serves:2
Ingredients Quantity
Maggie(small) 2 pckts
Water 2 glasses
Chilli powder(optional) 1 tsp
Cheese 1 slice
Mozarella cheese(optional) small cube

Steps for making:-

Step 1:Firstly in a large kadai take 2 glasses of water and boil it.
Step 2:After the water boils add 2 pckts of maggi and the masala.Mix it and let it boil for atleast about 2 minutes.
Step 3:After that add some chilli powder and add one slice of cheese again mix it and keep it on the stove for about 1 minute.Add some water if required.
Step 4:Now off the stove and serve it onto a plate.Add some mozarella cheese on the top for more cheesy maggie.
Step 5:The dish is ready.Serve it hot and enjoy

Recipe #3 Coffee
